The Echolalia Project aims to promote data collection, sharing, and analyzing of behavioral data to study language development in infants. The project focuses on creating community-improved, open-source, state-of-the-art Machine Learning tools to securely store, manage, analyze and share daylong audio and related data while respecting the privacy of the families.
